Welcome to MoodCat.me

Hi there! Welcome to MoodCat.me, the music platform of the future, available today. Join us in the adventure of finding and enjoying music geared to you! We try to understand you

How does it work?

Go to the homepage, and select what mood(s) you're in. Once you click next, we recommend you a couple of rooms to check out. Pick one you think you like, and you will join the room. We'll start playing music for you, and you can chat with people in the same mood as you are!

This song doesn't fit the room, why am I hearing this?

We're constantly trying to improve the system. You can give us feedback to help us. If you think a song fits the current mood, click the button in the room. This way, we know whe made the right recommendation. Similarly, if the song does not fit the room, click the button. We'll try to modify the system based on your feedback. If you want to help us even better, you can use the popup to modify the rating of the song to your vision.

We need your help!

Want to make MoodCat a better place? You can help us improving Moodcat in a couple of ways. All users can participate in our rating game. Listen to 30 seconds of music, and give your feedback on the song. For every 5 songs you rate, you earn 30 points!

But there are even more ways to earn points. If you find a bug in MoodCat, file a Github issue!. Correct and useful bug reports can earn you up to 100 points!

Have you thought of a cool new feature, and do you want to share it with everybody? If you submit a Pull-Request, we see the feature as fun/useful, and your code complies with our standards, we merge your PR and you earn up to 250 points!
